About Transcend Group

Transcend Group is a cross-border consulting practice that supports startups, technology companies, and international businesses looking to expand into North America.

We specialize in helping organizations navigate the complexities of entering a new market— including business strategy, partnership development, operational setup, and market positioning.

Our role is to bridge the gap between regions by combining business insight, data-driven analysis, and real-world understanding of how companies scale in competitive environments.

Whether you are a startup validating your first international expansion or an established company exploring North American growth, we help you move from idea → strategy → execution.
